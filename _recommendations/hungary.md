## Budapest

- Thermal baths - good all year as it has natural hot water with healing properties - either [Gellért Thermal Bath](https://maps.app.goo.gl/WWrpR4bteTM2YyrK8) or [Széchenyi Thermal Bath](https://maps.app.goo.gl/CCAua14x5VryxzzA7) depending on where you are
- Széchenyi Thermal Bath is on the Pest side around [Heroes' Square](https://maps.app.goo.gl/KEpy9HwgP7aPeEqu7), which also has art museums around it like [Museum of Fine Arts](https://maps.app.goo.gl/JVKLmkfQjYRQtvi47), the [City Park Ice Rink & Boating](https://maps.app.goo.gl/GggeEPPX6X1c2LHg7), depending on season and big park with a recreated [Vajdahunyad Castle](https://maps.app.goo.gl/7ySxTcmiskLE33Yt8)
- Gellért Thermal Bath is on the Buda side next to [Gellert Hill](https://maps.app.goo.gl/4YKqizDaeiG6SkS56), which can be good to climb for a nice view of the city. The street that leads up to it, Bartók Béla út, has hip cafes and bars, like my friend's vietnamese [Caphe by Hai Nam](https://maps.app.goo.gl/BPeh1mGz9cqSpAjw6), so can eat/drink around there.
- Crossing the river to the Pest side from Gellért Thermal Bath, you have the [Central Market Hall](https://maps.app.goo.gl/QREdPNjgJFM1xipA9), which is a nice market and you can walk from there on Váci utca, to [Vörösmarty tér](https://maps.app.goo.gl/dxwGyaCSV74Z31RH9) which is a restaurant/shopping street with nice buildings and can also go down to the riverside.
- Also around there is the Jewish corner, with cool pubs and nice to walk around, like the most famous ruin pub [Szimpla Kert](https://maps.app.goo.gl/eEdr6F1KhrMbQB1x9), which is touristy, but still definitley worth a drink/visit, some restaurants like [Gettó Gulyás](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ySK7b35S9Sy4Ykkh7), which serves hungarian food and places around [Gozsdu Court](https://maps.app.goo.gl/kdH3QrrutqwCgn7cA).
- Also around there you have [St. Stephen's Basilica](https://maps.app.goo.gl/vCByioSBHPAwgjdv6) that has wine bars and restaurants around (although expensive). For hungarian food [Retek Bisztro](Retek Bisztro) looks nice.
- Crossing back to the Buda side on the [Széchenyi Chain Bridge](https://maps.app.goo.gl/LaoVA2jR33znuAPBA), you have the Castle hill, with [Buda Castle](https://maps.app.goo.gl/dAHMiRZGrvAyN4K76) and [Fisherman's Bastion](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Ek5yNvy6bCbnqSsTA), both of which are worth walking around.
- Some miscallenous drinks/food places
    - [Élesztő](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Krn9unWbgw1cDnpV9) - craft beer and simple food
    - [The Grund](https://maps.app.goo.gl/qPjfH2ujiiJQyrR86) - large indoord/outoor pub
    - [Bambi Café](https://maps.app.goo.gl/SkZuNkEZswaNW9QY7) - a socialist looking cafe/bar that has not changed in 50 years
    - [Hai Nam Pho Bistro](https://maps.app.goo.gl/t9qQVmUmUUTkB9hX8) - friend's vietnamese restaurant
    - [Gólya Presszó](https://maps.app.goo.gl/batHVnw2fvuJ2XUf7) - not sure if anything is around it though

- [Pécs](https://maps.app.goo.gl/deprt68o7AW14vmWA), never been, but heard good things
