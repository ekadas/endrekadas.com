## Ingredients

* 900g potatoes
* 2 large sweet onions
* 2 * 50g anchovies in olive oil
* 450ml whipping cream half and half heavy and single cream

## Directions

* Preheat oven to 200 C
* Cut potatoes into matchstick strips, put half of them into greased baking dish
* Slice onions into rings and lay half of them on the potatoes
* Lay the anchovies on top of the onion, then add onions and potatoes
* Mix the cream with 30ml of water and pour over the dish
* Cover with foil and cook for 1 hour
* Reduce temperature to 180 C and uncover dish and cook for a further 45 mins
