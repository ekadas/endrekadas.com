## Ingredients
* eggs - 3pc
* flour - 150g/1.5-2 cups
* kefir - 100g/0.5 cups
* boiled water - 100g/0.5 cups
* warm milk - 1 pint
* sugar - 20g/1-2 spoons
* vegetable oil - 30g/1 spoon
* soda - 0.25 teaspoon
* salt - 0.5 teaspoon

## Directions

1. In a large container mix the eggs, sugar and salt.
1. Add the kefir and half of the milk and mix.
1. Add the boiled water very slowly and continously mixing, so that it doesn't boil the other ingredients.
1. Add the flour slowly and mixing it so that we don't have any knots.
1. Add the soda.
1. Add the rest of the milk, to make it not too dense.
2. Add the vegetable oil - mix is done!
